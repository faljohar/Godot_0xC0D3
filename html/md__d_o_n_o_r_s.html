Donors to the Godot Engine project

Godot Engine is a non-profit project developed by a community of voluntary contributors, as well as occasional paid contributors thanks to the financial support of generous donors.

The ways to donate to the project, as well as details on how the funds are used, are described on Godot's website.

The following is a list of the current monthly donors, to be have their generous deed immortalized in the next stable release of Godot Engine.
